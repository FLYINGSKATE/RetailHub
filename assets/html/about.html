About Us

Welcome to FlutterMeet! We are thrilled to introduce our innovative communication app that allows you to connect with all your teams, be they family, friends or colleagues, in an instant.

Our app is designed to make video conferencing efficient and adapting to your scale, providing an easy way to communicate with anyone, anywhere, at any time.

Using the power of Jitsi SDK, an open-source technology, we are able to offer high-quality video and audio conferences with encryption by default.

With FlutterMeet, you can have unlimited users, making it the perfect option for businesses, remote teams, and personal use.

At FlutterMeet, our goal is to make communication easy and accessible for everyone. We believe that by simplifying the process of setting up and attending virtual meetings, we can help to increase productivity and collaboration.

Thank you for choosing FlutterMeet, we look forward to connecting you with the world.
